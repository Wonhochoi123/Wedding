About Mikayla

When I was 20 I moved from a small town in GA to the west coast within a week of making the decision.
I've driven across North America, jumped out of airplanes, tumbled down hills on snowboards,
attempted to hike well, swam in freezing waters, camped in very questionable places,
endured MANY sunburns, and decided to learn a new language.
The last one is the reason I am here today and is the greatest choice I've made so far.
Here's to more adventures--

Our Story

Learn about our relationship story!

4 August 2020

When We First Met

The boredom created by the pandemic was painful enough to motivate us to find a new hobbie: Korean tutoring. As I was a student and Wonho just began tutoring again, we found each other. After a few lessons and many, many off the topic conversations, we decided to see each other outside of the tutoring.

4 June 2021

Family Visit

After almost a year of dating, Mikayla and I decided to visit her family for the summer secretely intending to get the blessing from her parents for marriage.
A few days into the visit, I anxiously asked Mikayla's dad for his approval, and he answered me with 'yes' under one condition: learning how to shoot a gun + telling Mikayla that he said no.

16 June 2021

Proposal

One day, before going to meet my mom for lunch, Wonho and I decided to visit the lake so I could show him more of where I grew up. As we were hiking around the lake I noticed that Wonho was being EXTREMELY awkward and trying to find "good scenery" and that's when he got on one knee and blinded me with the most beautiful ring I have ever seen. Nothing in my humble 24 year life could have made me happier.

17 June 2021

Elopement

Surprise! We eloped. After witnessing many engagements without an end in sight we decided to elope on the condition of having a ceremony for both our families within the next couple years. This is how we went from dating, engaged, to married within three days. So please join us for one of our three ceremonies.
